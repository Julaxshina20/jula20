Fast Company

Fast Company is business and finance magazine that keeps managers, entrepreneurs and business owners abreast of current business trends and ideas.

Since the first issue was published in 1995, Fast Company has been considered the go-to guide for entrepreneurs that focuses on business innovation in relation to creativity, leadership, economics, design, and the ever-changing technology world.

Popular features that are found in every issue include the following: "Now," which is a collection of short articles and news brief; "Next," which offers a look at innovative qualities such as technology, philanthropic support, and design in the business world; "Wanted," showcasing Editors' picks when it comes to must-have gadgets; and "Fast Talk," which is an assessment of everything from business turnaround to important technologies for entrepreneurs. Each issue encourages readers and leaders to embrace the changing business and technology world with bold ideas that help set the stage for future business. Fast Company is published six times every year, along with a number of special issues. Possible themes may include "World-Changing Innovations," "Most Innovative Companies," "The Technology Special," "The 100 Most Creative People in Business," "Innovation by Design," "How to Lead a Creative Life," and "Secrets of the Most Productive People."
